## Business Workflow

- **Task**: Automatically generate supply chain delay notification emails
- **User**: Supply chain coordinators who need to notify customers quickly
- **Input**: Delay reason, estimated delay days, customer name, product name
- **Output**: A professional, concise customer notification email
- **Value**: Reduces email drafting time from ~5 minutes to under 30 seconds
  per email, while maintaining consistent tone and format
